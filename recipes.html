1. Choco-chip Cookie

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Granulated sugar
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla extract
  • Egg
  • Baking soda

Recipe:

  1. Melt the butter. The butter shouldn’t be sizzling hot.
  2. Whisk in the sugars until well-combined.
  3. Mix in the vanilla extract and the egg.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in just until combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips, then scoop the dough onto a baking sheet.
  6. Bake until golden brown!

Enjoy your delicious choco-chip cookies!

Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Recipe:

Follow the following steps-

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tray with paper liners.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in a bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. In another bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with the milk. Mix until smooth.
  7. Spoon the batter evenly into the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let the cupcakes cool before frosting.

Frosting (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tbsp milk
  • A pinch of salt

Mix the ingredients to make a creamy frosting and frost the cupcakes after they have cooled.

Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Recipe:

Follow the following steps-

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the mashed bananas, eggs, vanilla extract, and sour cream. Mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the banana bread to cool before slicing.
